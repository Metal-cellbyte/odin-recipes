Hamburger

Description

Made with a patty of ground beef, seasoned with salt and pepper. Topped with lettuce, tomato, onions, and of course pickles. Put together to make a simple and savory meal..

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef.
  • Salt and pepper.
  • 2 hamburger buns.
  • Tomato slices.
  • Pickle slices.
  • Iceberg lettuce.
  • Onion, red or white, thinly sliced.

Steps

  1. Combine salt and pepper in a bowl. Set aside
  2. Form patties. Divide ground beef into desired portions and gorm into 1/2 inch thick patties. Cover and set aside
  3. Sprinkle salt and pepper before cooking. Place on hot grill or pan. Cook each side for 3-4 minutes or until beef reaches 160 degrees F.
  4. Remove patties and place in buns, with toppings.
