Tacos

Description

A meal that can be made within 30 minutes, a perfect weeknight meal.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Oil
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef until it's completely browned. Drain the grease, and add seasoning.
  2. Let taco meat to simmer briefly in sauce
  3. Fry the tortillas in a small amount of oil in a medium size skillet.Remove and fold in half.
  4. Fill the taco shell with the cooked beef and any toppings desired.
