Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza is an American pizza variety which includes one of the country's most beloved toppings. Usually made with a mixture of beef, pork, and spices.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F.
  2. Make sauce of choice.
  3. Move dough to a floured surface: knead until smooth and elastic.
  4. Pat dough onto prerpared pizza pan.
  5. Top dough with sauce, cheese, and pepperoni.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven, 18 to 20 minutes.
